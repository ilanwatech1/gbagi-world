About Mrs. Ruth Onoja

Mrs. Ruth Onoja is a passionate gospel musician whose mission is to inspire lives through the transformative power of music.

Her journey began at a young age, singing in her local church choir, where she discovered her love for gospel music.

"Let everything that has breath praise the Lord." - Psalm 150:6

Join Mrs. Onoja on this beautiful journey of faith and music, and let the melodies of praise fill your heart and soul.
